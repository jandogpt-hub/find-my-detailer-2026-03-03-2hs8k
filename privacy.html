2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect:

  • Through the Find My Detailer website and any related subdomains.
  • When you browse the directory, search for detailers, or view listings.
  • When you create, claim, or manage a directory listing.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected by third parties that may be linked to or integrated with our Website, including external websites or services that you access through links on our Website.

3. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with the Website. This may include information that identifies you or can be associated with you ("Personal Information") and information that does not identify you ("Non-Personal Information").

3.1 Information Collected from Directory Listings

When a business or service provider is listed in the directory, we may collect and display information about that listing, including:

  • Business or trading name
  • Business category and services offered (e.g., detailing, washing, polishing)
  • Physical address or service area (city, region, country)
  • Contact information such as phone number, email address, website URL, and social media profiles
  • Business description, photos, and other marketing content provided to us
  • Operating hours and pricing information (where applicable)
  • Location data (city, region) for search and filtering purposes
  • Any other information voluntarily submitted as part of the listing (e.g., certifications, specialties)

Some listing information may be collected from the business owner, an authorized representative, public sources, or third-party data providers. We use this information to maintain an accurate and useful directory service.

3.2 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account or profile information (if applicable): such as your name, email address, password, and role (e.g., business owner, manager).
  • Contact inquiries: information you provide when you contact us via email or forms, including your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Claiming or managing a listing: identity verification details, business ownership details, and any updates or corrections you submit.
  • Feedback and reviews (if enabled): information you provide in reviews, ratings, or feedback regarding listed businesses.

3.3 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit or use the Website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Referring and exit pages, and the pages or content you view
  • Dates and times of access, and approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Clickstream data, search queries, and interactions with the Website

We typically use this information in aggregated or pseudonymized form to operate and improve the Website.

3.4 Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive Personal Information (such as health data, financial account numbers, or information about your racial or ethnic origin) through the Website. Please do not submit such information to us.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and provide the directory and related services.
  • To publish, display, and promote directory listings to users searching for detailers and related services.
  • To personalize user experience and show relevant listings based on location and preferences.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide support.
  • To verify and manage listing ownership and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized use.
  • To analyze how the Website is used and improve functionality, performance, and content.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our systems and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve the Website.

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to remember your preferences, enhance user experience, and provide aggregated data for analytics.

  • Performance and analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors use the Website, which pages are visited, and how users interact with our content.
  • Functionality cookies: allow the Website to remember your preferences, such as language or location settings.

6.3 Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the Website.
